# Welcome to Vanguard! 👋

Vanguard is an AI-powered component mutation engine for Next.js projects. In seconds, you can describe how you want your component to change, and Vanguard will generate the code for you.

## Sign In

Let's get you started by creating or signing into your Vanguard account. Your account is free for the first 20 mutations per month.

**What happens next:**
1. A login panel will open
2. Enter your email
3. You'll get a secure session token
4. It's stored in VS Code SecretStorage (uses your OS keyring when available)

For account and data-handling details, refer to the project's privacy policy and terms.

👉 **Ready?** Click the "Sign In" button above to get started!
